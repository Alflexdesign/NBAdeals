Safari Booking Policies

Transparent policies designed to protect our travelers and build lifelong safari relationships.

Booking Policy +

All safari bookings with Nature Breeze Safaris are subject to confirmation and availability.

  • A deposit of 30% of the total safari cost is required to confirm a reservation.
  • The remaining balance must be paid 30 days before the safari start date.
  • If a safari is booked within 30 days of travel, full payment is required at booking.
  • Bookings are confirmed once deposit payment and confirmation invoice are issued.
  • All bookings must be made in writing via email or through our official booking platform.

This structure allows us to secure lodges, park permits, and transport arrangements for your safari.

Payment Policy +

We aim to make payments convenient for international and local travelers.

  • Payments can be made via bank transfer, credit/debit card, or mobile payment options.
  • International travelers may pay in USD or EUR.
  • Local travelers may pay via M-Pesa or bank transfer.
  • All bank transfer charges are the responsibility of the client.
  • Prices may change due to government park fee adjustments or currency fluctuations until booking is confirmed.

Once full payment is received, a final safari itinerary and travel confirmation will be issued.

Cancellation & Refund Policy +

We understand that travel plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to remain fair while covering costs already committed to lodges, transport, and park reservations.

  • 60 days or more before safari – Full refund minus deposit and third-party reservation fees.
  • 30–59 days before safari – 50% refund of total safari cost.
  • Less than 30 days before safari – No refund due to commitments already made.
  • No-show – 100% cancellation fee.

Refunds are processed within 14–30 business days using the original payment method.

We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unexpected cancellations.

Booking Amendment Policy +

We understand plans can evolve. Whenever possible we will assist in adjusting your safari itinerary.

  • Changes requested more than 30 days before departure are generally free of charge.
  • Changes within 30 days of departure may incur administrative or accommodation adjustment fees.
  • All amendments are subject to lodge availability.
  • If a requested change increases costs (e.g., upgraded accommodation), the difference will apply.

Our team will always try to find flexible solutions for our travelers.

Privacy Policy +

We respect the privacy of our travelers and are committed to protecting personal information.

  • Personal information collected includes name, contact information, passport details, and travel preferences.
  • This information is used strictly to arrange safari bookings, accommodations, and transportation.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties except for travel arrangements.
  • Payment information is processed through secure payment systems.
  • Travel photos may be used for marketing only with traveler permission.
Liability & Responsibility Policy +

Nature Breeze Safaris acts as an intermediary between travelers and third-party service providers such as lodges, airlines, and transport providers.

  • We are not liable for delays, accidents, natural disasters, or government actions beyond our control.
  • Safari itineraries may change due to weather, wildlife movement, or operational conditions.
  • In such cases, equivalent services will be provided whenever possible.
Force Majeure Policy +

Force majeure refers to unforeseen circumstances beyond our control including:

  • Natural disasters
  • Pandemics
  • Political unrest
  • Government restrictions
  • Airline disruptions

In such cases, we will prioritize rescheduling your safari or issuing travel credit for future use.
