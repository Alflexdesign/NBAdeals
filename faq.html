Kenya Safari FAQs

Everything you need to know before your Kenya safari adventure.

What is the best time to go on a safari in Kenya? +

The best time for a Kenya safari is during the dry seasons from June to October and January to March when wildlife is easier to spot around water sources. July to October is also famous for the Great Migration.

What animals can you see on a Kenya safari? +

Kenya is home to the famous Big Five: lions, elephants, buffalo, leopards, and rhinos. You can also see giraffes, zebras, cheetahs, wildebeest, hippos, and hundreds of bird species across different national parks.

How many days do you need for a Kenya safari? +

Most travelers choose a safari lasting 3 to 7 days. This allows time to explore top parks like Maasai Mara, Amboseli, Lake Nakuru, or Tsavo while enjoying multiple game drives.

How much does a Kenya safari cost? +

Safari prices vary depending on accommodation and duration. Budget safaris may start around $200 per day while luxury safaris can exceed $1000 per day including accommodation, park fees, and guided game drives.

What should I pack for a safari in Kenya? +

Pack lightweight neutral-colored clothing, a hat, sunscreen, insect repellent, comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, and a camera with zoom lens for wildlife photography.

Is Kenya safe for safari tourists? +

Yes. Kenya is one of Africa’s most popular safari destinations. Most safaris are conducted with experienced guides in protected national parks and conservancies.

What is a game drive on safari? +

A game drive is a guided wildlife viewing experience in a safari vehicle. Drives usually happen early morning and late afternoon when animals are most active.

Can you combine a safari with a beach holiday in Kenya? +

Yes. Many visitors combine wildlife safaris with beach destinations like Diani or Watamu for relaxation after exploring Kenya’s national parks.

Do I need vaccinations for a Kenya safari? +

Travelers are usually advised to consult a doctor about vaccinations and malaria prevention before visiting Kenya. Travel insurance is also recommended.
