Kenya Expeditions

Legendary Safari & Coastal Experiences

3 Days Masai Mara Safari

Embark on the ultimate Masai Mara safari adventure, a true jewel of Kenyan wildlife. Witness the legendary plains that host the world-famous Great Wildebeest Migration. Spot the majestic Big Five—lions, leopards, rhinos, elephants, and cape buffaloes—while enjoying unforgettable wildlife photography, authentic Maasai cultural encounters, and breathtaking hot air balloon safaris floating over the scenic Mara River. An unparalleled African safari experience awaits.

3 Days Amboseli Safari

Experience Amboseli National Park, renowned as the Land of Giants, where massive elephant herds roam freely beneath the breathtaking, iconic snow-capped peaks of Mount Kilimanjaro. Explore diverse habitats including lush wetlands, expansive dry lake beds, and woodlands teeming with incredible birdlife. This premium safari destination offers some of the most spectacular wildlife viewing and landscape photography opportunities in all of Kenya.

BOOK NOW

3 Days Lake Naivasha

Enjoy a tranquil Rift Valley escape at Lake Naivasha, a shimmering freshwater haven. Delight in guided boat safaris floating among resident pods of hippos and discover a birdwatching paradise boasting over 400 vibrant species. Combine relaxation with thrilling adventure hikes through the dramatic gorges and towering terrestrial cliffs of the nearby Hell's Gate National Park, one of Kenya's most unique geological wonders.

BOOK NOW

2 Days Lake Nakuru

Discover the vibrant beauty of Lake Nakuru, world-famous for the breathtaking spectacle of thousands of vivid pink flamingos beautifully painting the alkaline lake shores. Go on an exhilarating game drive to spot majestic black and white rhinos thriving in Kenya’s premier rhino conservation sanctuary. This picturesque, wildlife-rich destination provides a perfect compact safari packed with exceptional ecological diversity and thrilling natural encounters.

BOOK NOW

3 Days Tsavo East

Journey into the vast expanses of Tsavo East National Park, Kenya’s largest and oldest wilderness sanctuary. Known globally for its legendary "red elephants" dusted with the region's rich volcanic soil, Tsavo offers dramatic, untamed landscapes. Explore iconic landmarks such as the cascading Lugard Falls and the monumental Yatta Plateau, experiencing the ultimate off-the-beaten-path safari adventure full of raw, majestic African nature.

BOOK NOW

4 Days Diani Beach

Escape to the pristine shores of Diani Beach, consistently ranked among Africa’s absolute best coastal destinations. Relax on powdery white sands lined with lush palm trees and bathed by crystal-clear, warm turquoise waters. Indulge in luxury oceanfront resorts, thrilling snorkeling and scuba diving adventures in vibrant coral reefs, world-class kitesurfing, and rich, lively coastal nightlife under the starlit Indian Ocean sky.

2 Days Watamu Marine Park

Immerse yourself in the unspoiled tropical beauty of Watamu Marine National Park. Explore spectacular coral gardens teeming with colorful marine life, pristine snorkeling reefs, and tranquil coastal waters perfect for eco-friendly adventures. Journey back in time exploring the mysterious, ancient Gedi ruins tucked within lush forests, or enjoy peaceful mangrove kayaking in one of Kenya’s most breathtaking and ecologically diverse marine paradises.

BOOK NOW

3 Days Malindi Coast

Experience the enchanting blend of rich Swahili heritage and vibrant Italian coastal culture in stunning Malindi. Uncover centuries of history by visiting the historic Vasco da Gama pillar and exploring bustling local markets filled with authentic crafts and spices. Relax on sun-drenched, palm-fringed beaches and dive into pristine, thriving marine parks, making Malindi an unforgettable destination for culture enthusiasts and beach lovers alike.

Nairobi National Park

Discover Nairobi National Park, the only wildlife park in the world uniquely located within a bustling capital city. Experience the surreal thrill of watching wild lions, endangered rhinos, and towering giraffes roaming freely against the striking backdrop of Nairobi’s modern city skyline. A perfect, quick escape offering spectacular game drives, rich grass plains, and an unforgettable introduction to Kenya’s unmatched wildlife heritage.

BOOK NOW

National Museum

Immerse yourself in Kenya’s rich, multifaceted heritage at the Nairobi National Museum. Delve into fascinating exhibits taking you through millions of years of human evolution with rare fossil discoveries. Explore captivating tribal culture galleries showcasing diverse Kenyan traditions, marvel at comprehensive wildlife exhibits, and browse stunning contemporary African art portraying the dynamic history, culture, and incredible natural diversity of the country.

BOOK NOW

Giraffe Centre

Get up close and personal with nature at the iconic Giraffe Centre in Nairobi. Enjoy the unforgettable, hands-on experience of hand-feeding and interacting with the rare, endangered Rothschild giraffes from a raised wooden observation platform. Learn about vital wildlife conservation and breeding efforts dedicated to protecting these majestic, gentle giants and their natural habitats across Kenya.

BOOK NOW

Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage

Witness the heartwarming and inspiring conservation work at the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust Elephant Orphanage. Watch rescued and orphaned baby elephants and rhinos being lovingly rehabilitated, fed, and cared for by dedicated keepers. Learn about the crucial anti-poaching efforts and the complex journey of successfully reintroducing these incredibly intelligent and magnificent creatures back into their natural wild habitats.
